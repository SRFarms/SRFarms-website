Coconut Cultivation

Our main agricultural focus is sustainable, pesticide-free coconut farming where we cycle natural nutrients and utilize solar dome drying.

Step 1

Coconut Groves

Our groves feature tall, mature coconut palms cultivated chemical-free, yielding rich, sweet coconuts.

Step 2

Natural Fertilization

Fallen coconut fronds (leaves) are recycled as organic fertilizer/compost to enrich our soil naturally.

Step 3

Soil Mushrooms

Nature's proof of rich soil health: wild mushrooms growing naturally across our organic grounds.

Step 4

Solar Dome Dryer

Processing coconuts hygienically in a high-efficiency solar dome dryer to produce clean, premium copra.

Mango Varieties

The 2 types of Mango we cultivate are Panchavarnam and Kili Mooku.

Panchavarnam

Named after its beautiful five colors, this variety offers a unique sweet flavor with a rich, fibrous texture that is highly cherished in Tamil Nadu.

Flavor: Rich Sweet & Fibrous Season: April – June

Kili Mooku

Also known as Totapuri or parrot-beaked mango, it has a distinct curved shape, slightly sour-sweet crunchy taste when raw, and sweet juicy pulp when ripe.

Flavor: Sour-Sweet Juicy Season: April – May

Mango Cultivation

Our step-by-step mango harvest process, documented with original photos and videos directly from Saptur.

Step 1

Sun-Ripened Orchard Maturity

Panchavarnam and Kili Mooku mangoes maturing naturally on the branches, hanging in heavy clusters ready for selective handpicking.

Step 2

Traditional Pole Harvesting

Harvesting high-hanging fruits carefully using a traditional pole plucker equipped with a net basket to prevent bruising and drop damage.

Step 3

Paddy Straw Bedding

Arranging freshly picked green mangoes on dry paddy straw inside a clean, aerated chamber to initiate natural organic ripening.

Step 4

Traditional Hay Curing

Covering the stacked mangoes with hay to capture natural ethylene gas, ensuring uniform ripening over 3 to 5 days without chemical gases.

Step 5

Premium Harvest Readiness

The final golden, aromatic Panchavarnam and Kili Mooku mangoes, ready to be packed and delivered fresh from the groves of SR Farms.
